WHAT IS INSIDE "GROSS PROFIT"
✓ The gross margin % IS covering
COGS · manufacturing · raw materials · inbound freight · packaging · shipping to customer · payment gateway fees · returns & refunds · warehousing · marketplace fees
Gross profit = Revenue × Margin % − Total ad spend
✗ NOT included (fixed costs)
Agency fees · tech stack (Shopify, Klaviyo) · office rent & salaries · founder salary · brand & creative production · corporate taxes · interest & finance charges
Fixed costs are paid regardless of ad spend, so they don't belong in the "is the next ₹ of ad spend worth it?" decision. That's why gross profit (not net profit) is the right number here.

Model assumptions: Performance revenue follows a diminishing-returns curve with elasticity ≈ 0.76 (+10% spend → +7.5% revenue, +20% spend → +15% revenue, +30% spend → +22.5% revenue). Awareness contribution grows as √spend (heavier diminishing returns, because awareness takes longer to convert and has a 60–90 day real-world lag). Gross profit = Revenue × Margin % − Total spend.

This is a model, not a forecast. It exists to make the shape of the argument visible. Plug in your own numbers above to stress-test the story against your account.
